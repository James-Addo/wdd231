Website Plan

Site Name

Ghana Travel & Tour Company

The name represents a central platform for travelers to explore and experience Ghana's rich culture and beauty.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information on Ghana's attractions, travel tips, and services for tourists, including accommodation booking and tour planning, to promote Ghana as a leading travel destination, while supporting local businesses and communities.

Scenarios

  • What are the best places to visit in Ghana?
  • What are the most popular festivals and events in Ghana?
  • How do I plan a customized tour package for my family?
  • How can I book a hotel or accommodation in Ghana?

Color Scheme

  • Primary-color: Dark blue (#120A8F) – Will be used for heading and body text
  • Secondary-color: Engineering Orange (#BA160C) – Will be used for headings, body text and buttons
  • Background-color1: Light Grayish Orange (#f3edea) – Will be used as the background for the header
  • Background-color2: WhiteSmoke (#F5F5F5) – Will be used as the main background color.

Typography

  • Heading-font: Roboto – Roboto Regular 400 – To be used as font for headings
  • Text-font: Lato – Lato Light 300 – To be used as font for body text
